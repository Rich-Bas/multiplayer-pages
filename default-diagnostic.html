Most businesses are not badly designed. They are undesigned. They run on defaults, the ways of working that were inherited rather than chosen, and the most expensive of them are invisible. This is a ten minute diagnostic that finds the defaults likely costing you, and tells you which one is worth switching first.

No sign-up to start. No score to game. Just a sharper look at your own building.

Step three  /  Where defaults hide

The machine underneath

Your symptoms point to defaults across four layers. The taller the bar, the more of what you flagged traces back to that layer. Underneath each one are the specific defaults we would suspect.

Step four  /  Your read

How much, how stuck

We have made a first guess for each suspected default. Correct us. For every one, how much is it costing you, and how entrenched is it. This is what separates a default worth switching from one worth leaving alone.
